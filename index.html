Sri Lanka has had too many tragic accidents at rail road crossings that just cannot be ignored.

"There are 750 unprotected railroad crossings in Sri Lanka" - The Sunday Times

Many accidents at unprotected railroad crossings occur due to poor visibility or inadequate signs

Rail Senze is a simple yet powerful mobile application that alerts drivers of on coming railroad crossings

Our mission is to keep you and you family safe while driving on the road!

Key Features:

  • Uses Geo-fence technology to register all railroad crossings in the country
  • Supports Android and iOS mobile devices
  • Early warning depending on the speed of the vehicle
  • Unique warning levels to the driver based on:
    • The existence or non-existence of gates at the crossing
      (e.g. Red alert at unprotected crossings)
    • Past history of accidents at a particular crossing
  • Unique visual and audio alerts depending on the risk level
  • Crowdsourcing feature which lets drivers report unexpected dangers at crossings
  • Offline capability, specify your route before you start your journey and download relevant alerts
